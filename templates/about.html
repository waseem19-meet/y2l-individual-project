About

This website has been created by Waseem Lawen.
As a part of our journey throughout the program MEET: The Middle East Entrepreneurs of tommorrow, we have to submit a website created by each individual student, in our second year in the program.

This website hasn't been created just because we are obliged to in order for us to to continue in the program, but it was created because it's a very important topic that needs to be helped with because for me as a palestinian living in Israel at some point I'm going to attend a Hebrew speaking university and I may as well face the same difficulties other Palestinians have and are still facing because we don't get our right to mother-tounge education.


So I hope this website helps you!
